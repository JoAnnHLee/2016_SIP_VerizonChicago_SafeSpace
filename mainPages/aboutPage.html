About Safe Space


This project aims to promote public safety of solo wanderers. Walking alone can be dangerous, and our website Safe Space is created to limit the dangers of walking alone. Whenever our users has to travel through a dangerous area, they'll set their current location and final destination in our GPS map system. If our users do not reach the designated area in the estimated amount of time, our website will alert and ask our users if they are safe. If so, our users can simply exit the website. If not, the website will automatically send messages to our user's emergency contact after a certain period of time.
